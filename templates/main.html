What kinds of laundry products are there?

Pacs:

These are multi-chamber liquid detergents !! These are easy to use and still effective in cleaning, fighting stains, and brightening laundry !

Liquid:

Best for grease and oil stains !! When using liquid detergent, make sure to use the correct dosage. Read the care label instructions on your clothes !

Powder:

This is best for dirt or clay-like stains !! To use this, you can add it to the top loader, front loader, or the HE washer :3 you can also use this in place of any type of detergent. As long as it is machine washable, you can use this, or liquid detergents !

Stain Remover:

Before using stain remover, make sure to identify what type of stain you are dealing with, and make sure to read the instructions on the product label ! There are many different stain removers, such as spray, gel, paste, or powder !

Laundry Booster:

Laundry boosters are used to give clothes a cleaner and fresher feeling ! Use these when you have really soiled items, are using hard water (this can help the detergent to work better ! ) or if your clothes are really stinky !

Fabric Care:

These are used in addition to regular laundry detergent for any specific needs or benefits ! These can help your clothes look better, feel better, and last longer !

Fabric Rinse:

This helps to remove any leftover detergent, make clothes feel softer, reduce any roughness, enhance fragrance, etc.

Laundry Tiles:

These are another type of detergent, like liquids or pods ! These are energy-saving, do not require any plastic (sustainability !!!) and are used in cold-water washing ! The fibers dissolve instantly and completely in the water !!
